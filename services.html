Our Services

Comprehensive facility management solutions tailored to your needs.

1. Housekeeping & Cleaning Services

People Serve India LLP delivers structured housekeeping and cleaning solutions designed to maintain hygienic, organized, and high-performance environments across commercial, residential, and industrial facilities.

Scope of Services

  • Daily and periodic cleaning of offices, lobbies, corridors, and common areas
  • Deep cleaning and sanitization services
  • Restroom hygiene management
  • Waste segregation and disposal management
  • Floor care (marble, granite, tile, wooden flooring)
  • Glass and façade cleaning
Our Value Proposition:
  • Trained & uniformed staff
  • Use of approved cleaning chemicals & equipment
  • Scheduled audits & quality checks
  • Compliance with hygiene and safety norms

2. Manpower Supply & Support Staff

We provide reliable, verified, and professionally trained manpower to support day-to-day operational requirements across multiple sectors. Our solutions are built on compliance, workforce discipline, and performance accountability.

Roles Deployed

  • Office Assistants
  • Facility Support Staff
  • Housekeeping Personnel
  • Administrative Support
  • Helper & Utility Staff
  • Multi-skilled Technicians
Operational Framework:
  • Background-verified personnel
  • Uniformed and trained workforce
  • Compliance with Labour Laws, PF, ESI
  • Performance monitoring & supervision
  • Replacement assurance policy

3. Security & Front-Office Assistance

Security is not just about guarding premises — it is about safeguarding assets, people, and reputation. We provide disciplined, trained security personnel and professional executives.

Security Services

  • Access control & visitor management
  • Surveillance monitoring
  • Patrolling & perimeter security
  • Emergency response handling

Front-Office Support

  • Reception management
  • Visitor coordination
  • Call handling
  • Administrative assistance
Value Proposition:
  • Licensed & trained security staff
  • Professional conduct & grooming
  • Incident escalation protocols
  • 24/7 operational readiness

4. Technical & Maintenance Support

Infrastructure Reliability & Preventive Maintenance. We ensure uninterrupted facility operations and infrastructure longevity through skilled personnel.

Technical Services
